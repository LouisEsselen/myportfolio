What Do I write?

I help businesses create high converting Landing pages to increase sales and conversions. The effectiveness of your landing pages are only as good as your "clearly defined and effective value proposition" for your company, products and services.

I'm familiar with the forex markets and the emini S&P500 futures market. As well as golf and golf instruction. This was my career for the past 33 years as a qualified PGA professional!

Evaluate & Articulate Your Value Proposition

 Nothing is more important than a clearly defined value proposition. It defines you, your company, and your products and services. Without it, you cannot communicate clearly with your audience.

Landing Page Messaging for Better Conversion

A well thought out message written in a sequence of thought for the reader, creates the best opportunity for increased conversions. Starting with a clearly defined "value proposition."

Evaluate and Manage your Data

Monitoring data is necessary for improving conversions and testing new pages over time.

Meet Louis


My passion for writing was ignited when I wrote and published a golf instruction book to Amazon in 2012.

In November 2022 I got serious about writing and started studying copywriting, specifically, direct response writing. I had exposure to this form of writing when I started my golf website in 2007 but never took it seriously at the time.

My recent Intense writing studies opened my eyes to a whole new world of understanding of what copy converts for better results. 

This lead me on a journey learning how to write effective landing page messaging to increase the conversion of the page. To do this, you need to have a clear concisely crafted value proposition for the message to be effective and convert the reader. 

I am offering my skills to help you achieve a higher rate of conversion for your landing pages. I'm a relatively new writer building my portfolio.
